# Developer Platform

#### Welcome to AsceSwap

**AsceSwap** is a decentralized protocol for interest rate swaps — the largest derivatives market in the world ($500T+ notional), built natively onchain.

Interest rate swaps are how the global economy manages the price of money. Banks hedge mortgage books with them. Corporations lock in borrowing costs. Traders bet on rate direction. This market has been inaccessible to most people — until now.

AsceSwap brings it to anyone with a wallet. No order books. No minimum sizes. No intermediaries.
